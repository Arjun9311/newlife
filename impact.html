A Shelter to Stay

Eliminating the feeling of loneliness and desertion, each child becomes a member of the Vishwashanthi Shrushti Seva Trust Home - a safe and secure environment with a genuine family atmosphere where children feel they belong.

Education Impact

All children above age five are registered in school. Every child at Brilliant Master School, Nagole studies with full supplies - uniforms, books, stationery - paid for by the Trust. Evening tuition, morning exercise, and weekend music classes round out a complete education.

Food and Health Impact

Children receive a balanced, home-cooked nutritious meal every day. Any health issue is addressed immediately by a local doctor.

HIV/AIDS Awareness Impact

Fighting India's Third Largest HIV/AIDS Crisis

India still has approximately 2.5 million people living with HIV/AIDS. The Trust's awareness programme aligns with NACO's National AIDS Control Programme by focusing on education, reducing stigma, and connecting people to ART services. India reduced new infections by 57% through sustained awareness efforts, and the Trust is proud to be part of that effort at the community level.

From 5 to 90

Growth that stayed faithful to the original promise.

We began with 5 children in September 2013. Today, 20 children call our Home their home, and 70 more are supported in their own communities. Every one of them has a safe place, a school, a meal, and people who care - because that is what we promised when we started.
