Who We Are

Care rooted in education, dignity, and steady daily support.

Newlife Foundation India is a not-for-profit social development organisation registered under the Indian Trust Act. Its work centres on the development and empowerment of women and children through education, health, child development, and livelihoods support. Registration No. 768K IV/2024 and REGD.NO: 2091/2001 reflect the Trust's standing with the Government of Telangana.

“To aid as many underprivileged persons as possible to get education, which for them would not otherwise be possible.”
Programmes Overview

Three of the ways the Trust serves every day.

Children's Home

Safe residential care for orphaned and semi-orphaned children aged 4 to 12, with schooling, shelter, and a genuine family atmosphere.

Learn More

Education Support

Uniforms, textbooks, notebooks, stationery, fees, evening tuition, morning exercise, and weekend music keep children learning without interruption.

Learn More

HIV/AIDS Awareness

Community education drives reduce stigma, share prevention knowledge, and connect people to government services and ART centres.

Learn More
School Highlight: Our children study at Brilliant Master School, Nagole, Hyderabad - enrolled, uniformed, and fully supported.
