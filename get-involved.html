You Can Make a Difference

No donation required. Your time and presence matter.

You could donate clothes, or you could join one of our visits. You are welcome to visit these children even on weekends or any holiday. Your visit builds confidence in these children. You could be a mentor by teaching them basic ethics, tips on hygiene, vocabulary, basic computer knowledge, handwriting, or how to read a map.

Visit Us

Come on any day - weekday or holiday. Spend time with the children. Play, talk, listen. Your presence alone makes a difference.

Be a Mentor

Teach basic ethics, hygiene, vocabulary, handwriting, how to read a map, or any life skill you have. Evening hours work well with the children's timetable.

Donate Clothes

Children aged 4 to 12 always need clothing. Bring clean, wearable clothes to the Trust Home directly. No courier is needed - just visit.

Teach Computer Skills

Basic computer knowledge opens doors. If you can spend an hour a week teaching, our children will benefit for a lifetime. The Trust is also looking for computer equipment donations in the form of hardware and devices.

Lead a Class

Volunteer to lead physical exercise in the morning, a music session on weekends, an art class, or a reading circle. The children follow a structured timetable and love scheduled activities.

Spread Awareness

Share the Trust's story with your friends, family, colleagues, and network. Awareness brings more mentors, more visitors, and more support without a single rupee spent.
